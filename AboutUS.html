Who are we?

I created Study Spot Finder as a simple tool to help other students find the best places to study. Whether it’s a quiet spot in the library, a chill café, or a table outside, I wanted to make it easier for people to discover the perfect place to focus. This site is a small project, but I hope it can grow into something even more helpful for students like me.
